Saturday, June 17- BYOM (Bring Your Own Meat)!!!!

                  Mark your Calendars!!!! 

Everyone's favorite event here at 3 P's in a Vine!   You bring your own meat to grill on our grills, you cook it yourself, and we do the rest!   We provide you a baked potato, salad, and all the fix'ns for the salad and potatoes.   Dinnerware, seasonings, and grills are here for your use.   There is no charge for this event, however we do hope you purchase some 3 P's in a Vine wine with your meal and maybe some to even take home.    


For this event, we do need you to RSVP so we have enough potatoes, salad, and tables.   We are limiting this event to approx. 60 people!   Make your reservations early and please let us know if you are canceling so we can get replacements.  We have our grills ready to go by 5:30 PM that day.  


RSVP by calling:   903-677-WINE(9463)  Leave a message with name and how many will be coming.   Also leave a phone number for us to reach you.   OR RSVP by Facebook or emailing us at:   pppinavine@yahoo.com

Saturday, June 24 -  Live Music -  Handpicked Ft. Mark Harrell and Jim McAdams (7:30PM - 10PM)


Come out and enjoy some great music and wine.   If you haven't heard these guys, you are in for a treat.  We will have them playing outside and they will entertain everyone with their covers and originals.   Plan to bring out your date or a bunch of friends to enjoy the evening with.   You might want to bring outdoor lawn chairs or a blanket to sit on.   This is going to be a great night.

Savor The Experience 

3 P's in a Vine is very excited to have won GOLD or 1st Place Texas Dry Red Wine at the Sulphur Springs 'Reds, Whites, and Brews Festival.   It was voted 'Hands Down' the best red wine by 4 Sommeliers that were flown in from around the country.  Our Cabernet Franc was the award winner.   Come out and give it a try.  You are sure to like it!

Brett and Rae Priestley, owners of 3 P's in a Vine, proudly display their 1st Place award.  The Cabernet Franc 2014, was the winning wine.   Stop out to 3 P's in a Vine and try some of their award winning wine.

And always here at 3 P's in a Vine, you can come out during all our hours of operation, and have a nice WINE TASTING, WINE BY THE GLASS, or WINE BY THE BOTTLE.  We will always welcome you here with our smiles and hospitality.   Come on out and have the WINE EXPERIENCE that YOU want!
